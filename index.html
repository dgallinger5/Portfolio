About Me

My Drive

Creating is where I truly come alive. Turning ideas to reality, planning, building, refining until it just feels right. Nothing fuels me more than a creative challenge and the process of shaping something great. I want to show my wife and kids that through passion and persistence you can build a life you love and enjoy, every day.

- ~ -

My Craft

I’ve been designing since childhood, scribbling ideas in the margins of homework, creating games for my sister and me to play, all the way to animating in 3ds Max. By middle school, I’d built a full website for a math project. In high school, I discovered digital audio and started producing music in FL Studio, which led me to study music production briefly in college. Afterward, I shifted focus to game design, learning Unity, then Unreal, and more recently experimenting with Godot. I’ve streamed parts of my creative process on Twitch using OBS with full audio, video, and lighting setups. Beyond digital games, I’ve game mastered multiple long-running Dungeons & Dragons campaigns, blending storytelling with design. These days, I bring ideas to life through Blender, Krita, and Procreate, crafting digital art that continues the same spirit I had as a kid, making worlds worth exploring.
